Data we support

Process parameters

Continuous, time‑based recordings per batch/line. Ideal for trend analysis, SPC and control optimization.

  • High‑frequency time series
  • Per‑batch & per‑line context
  • Outlier & drift detection

Quality attributes

Single measurement per batch for release and in‑process quality controls.

  • Spec windows & alerts
  • Batch comparisons
  • Correlation to process inputs

Stability data

Periodic measurements (e.g., annually) across product shelf life to ensure robustness.

  • Long‑horizon trends
  • Guideline‑aligned intervals
  • Forecasting & shelf‑life checks
